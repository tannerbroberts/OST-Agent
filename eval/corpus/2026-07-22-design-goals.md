Source: README + design spec, 2026-07-22

The goal is a tool a user downloads, runs one command, and then trusts to run completely
unmonitored while it tracks the accumulation of knowledge from the natural business
process into an Opportunity Solution Tree. Operators want to walk away and come back to a
tree that faithfully reflects what the business is learning, with fresh candidate solutions
and assumptions — without any unvalidated idea being presented as if it were proven.
